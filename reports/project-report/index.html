Other Implementations

We worked on many other features, not submitted for grading. They are listed here:

  • ENori: A Dear Gui-Version of Nori with a live-editable scene tree, written collaboratively (OpenGL parts by Matthias, efficient scene reloading by Roger)
  • Optix: GPU rendering and denoising with Nvidia GPUs, ~80% complete (all by Roger)
  • Euler: Our code once ran on Euler, but now with OpenVDB it does not anymore. But it worked back then. (Done by Matthias)
  • Directional Light: A simple light which has infinite distance but illuminates only a certain angles. This results in penumbras and is used for our final render image for the competition. (Matthias)
  • CMake: major refactor of CMake project, support for debugging, tested on windows and linux.
